# Lead Management

This document provides comprehensive documentation on the Lead Management feature of the CRM Web Application.

## Overview

The Lead Management feature helps sales teams track and manage potential sales opportunities from initial contact through qualification and conversion. It provides tools for capturing lead information, tracking interactions, assigning leads to sales representatives, and converting qualified leads into contracts.

## Key Concepts

### Lead Lifecycle

A typical lead goes through the following stages:

1. **New**: The lead has been created but no action has been taken
2. **Contacted**: Initial outreach has been made to the lead
3. **Qualified**: The lead has been assessed and determined to be a potential customer
4. **Proposal**: A formal business proposal has been presented to the lead
5. **Negotiation**: Terms are being negotiated with the lead
6. **Won**: The lead has been converted to a customer with a contract
7. **Lost**: The opportunity was not successful

### Lead Sources

Leads can come from various sources, including:

- Website inquiries
- Email campaigns
- Trade shows and events
- Referrals
- Cold calls
- Social media
- Marketing campaigns

## Creating a Lead

### As a Manager or Employee

1. Navigate to "Leads" > "Create Lead"
2. Fill in the required information:
   - Contact information (name, company, email, phone)
   - Lead source
   - Estimated value
   - Description and notes
   - Assigned sales representative
   - Lead status
3. Attach relevant documents (optional)
4. Click "Create Lead"

### Bulk Lead Import

For importing multiple leads at once:

1. Navigate to "Leads" > "Import Leads"
2. Download the template CSV file
3. Fill in the template with lead information
4. Upload the completed file
5. Review and confirm the import

## Viewing and Managing Leads

### Lead List View

1. Navigate to "Leads" > "All Leads" (managers) or "My Leads" (employees)
2. Use filters to narrow down the list:
   - Status
   - Source
   - Date range
   - Assigned representative
   - Value range

### Lead Detail View

Click on any lead to see detailed information:

1. Overview tab: Basic information and status
2. Activities tab: History of interactions
3. Documents tab: Attached files and notes
4. Tasks tab: Scheduled follow-up activities
5. Communications tab: Email history related to the lead

## Lead Assignment

### Manual Assignment

Managers can assign leads to specific employees:

1. Open the lead
2. Click "Edit"
3. Select an employee from the "Assigned To" dropdown
4. Click "Save"

### Automatic Assignment

The system can automatically assign leads based on rules:

1. Round-robin distribution
2. Based on workload
3. Based on expertise or territory
4. Based on lead source

Configure automatic assignment rules in "Settings" > "Lead Assignment Rules".

## Lead Activities and Follow-ups

### Recording Activities

Record interactions with leads:

1. Open the lead
2. Click "Add Activity"
3. Select activity type (call, email, meeting, etc.)
4. Enter activity details
5. Click "Save"

### Scheduling Follow-ups

Set reminders for future interactions:

1. Open the lead
2. Click "Schedule Follow-up"
3. Select follow-up type
4. Set date and time
5. Add notes
6. Click "Save"

## Google Integration Features

### Calendar Integration

Schedule meetings with leads directly from the lead detail page:

1. Open the lead
2. Click "Schedule Meeting"
3. Set meeting details
4. Add attendees
5. Click "Save"

The meeting will be added to your Google Calendar and linked to the lead.

### Email Integration

Send emails to leads from within the CRM:

1. Open the lead
2. Click "Send Email"
3. Compose your message
4. Attach files if needed
5. Click "Send"

Emails are sent through Gmail and recorded in the lead's communication history.

### Document Management

Store and share documents related to leads:

1. Open the lead
2. Click "Add Document"
3. Upload a file or select from Google Drive
4. Add a description
5. Set visibility (internal or shared with lead)
6. Click "Save"

## Lead Conversion

When a lead is ready to become a customer:

1. Open the lead
2. Click "Convert to Contract"
3. Fill in contract details:
   - Contract value
   - Start and end dates
   - Terms and conditions
   - Products or services
4. Click "Create Contract"
5. The lead status will be updated to "Won"

## Lead Analytics and Reporting

### Available Reports

1. **Lead Source Analysis**: Which sources generate the most leads
2. **Conversion Rates**: Percentage of leads that convert to customers
3. **Sales Pipeline**: Value and status of leads in the pipeline
4. **Lead Aging**: How long leads remain in each stage
5. **Rep Performance**: Comparison of team members' lead handling

### Generating Reports

1. Navigate to "Reports" > "Lead Reports"
2. Select the report type
3. Set filters and parameters
4. Click "Generate Report"
5. View the report or export it to Excel/CSV/PDF

## Email Notifications

The system can send email notifications for lead events:

- New lead creation
- Lead assignment
- Status changes
- Follow-up reminders
- Lead conversion

Configure notification preferences in your user settings.

## Best Practices for Lead Management

1. **Prompt Follow-up**: Respond to new leads within 24 hours
2. **Complete Information**: Capture as much relevant information as possible
3. **Consistent Updates**: Keep lead status and information current
4. **Document Interactions**: Record all communications with the lead
5. **Use Templates**: Utilize email templates for consistent messaging
6. **Regular Review**: Review aging leads to prevent them from going cold
7. **Qualification Process**: Establish clear criteria for qualifying leads
8. **Lead Scoring**: Implement a scoring system to prioritize high-value leads
