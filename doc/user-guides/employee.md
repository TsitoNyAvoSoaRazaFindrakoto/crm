# Employee User Guide

This guide provides comprehensive instructions for employees using the CRM Web Application. It covers all the features available to users with the Employee role.

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the CRM login page
2. Enter your username and password
3. Alternatively, click "Sign in with Google" to use your Google account
4. Click "Log In"

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with:

- Recent tickets assigned to you
- Upcoming meetings and appointments
- Tasks and reminders
- Performance metrics
- Recent activities

## Managing Your Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click on your username in the top-right corner
2. Select "My Profile"

### Updating Your Profile

1. Navigate to your profile
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update your information:
   - Contact details
   - Profile picture
   - Password
   - Notification preferences
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Connecting Google Services

1. Navigate to your profile
2. Click "Google Services"
3. Click "Connect" next to the service you want to enable:
   - Google Drive
   - Gmail
   - Google Calendar
4. Follow the prompts to authorize the CRM application

## Ticket Management

### Viewing Your Assigned Tickets

1. Navigate to "Tickets" > "My Tickets"
2. Use filters to sort and search tickets:
   - Status
   - Priority
   - Date created
   - Customer

### Creating a New Ticket

1. Navigate to "Tickets" > "Create Ticket"
2. Fill in the ticket details:
   - Customer
   - Subject
   - Description
   - Priority
   - Status
3. Attach files if needed
4. Click "Create Ticket"

### Updating a Ticket

1. Open the ticket you want to update
2. Click "Edit"
3. Update the relevant fields
4. Add comments to document your actions
5. Click "Save"

### Resolving a Ticket

1. Open the ticket you want to resolve
2. Update the status to "Resolved"
3. Add a comment describing the resolution
4. Click "Save"

## Lead Management

### Viewing Your Assigned Leads

1. Navigate to "Leads" > "My Leads"
2. Use filters to sort and search leads:
   - Status
   - Source
   - Date created
   - Potential value

### Creating a New Lead

1. Navigate to "Leads" > "Create Lead"
2. Fill in the lead details:
   - Customer information
   - Source
   - Description
   - Potential value
   - Status
3. Attach relevant documents
4. Click "Create Lead"

### Updating a Lead

1. Open the lead you want to update
2. Click "Edit"
3. Update the relevant fields
4. Document your interactions with the lead
5. Click "Save"

### Converting a Lead to a Contract

1. Open the lead
2. Click "Convert to Contract"
3. Fill in the contract details
4. Click "Create Contract"

## Contract Management

### Viewing Your Assigned Contracts

1. Navigate to "Contracts" > "My Contracts"
2. Use filters to sort and search contracts:
   - Status
   - Value
   - Start/end dates
   - Customer

### Creating a New Contract

1. Navigate to "Contracts" > "Create Contract"
2. Fill in the contract details:
   - Customer
   - Description
   - Value
   - Start and end dates
   - Terms and conditions
3. Attach relevant documents
4. Click "Create Contract"

### Updating a Contract

1. Open the contract you want to update
2. Click "Edit"
3. Update the relevant fields
4. Click "Save"

## Calendar and Meetings

### Viewing Your Calendar

1. Navigate to "Calendar"
2. Switch between day, week, and month views
3. Click on an event to view its details

### Creating a New Meeting

1. Navigate to "Calendar"
2. Click on the desired date and time slot
3. Fill in the meeting details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Location (physical or virtual)
   - Attendees
   - Linked ticket/lead/contract (optional)
4. Click "Save"

### Managing Meeting Invitations

1. Navigate to "Calendar" > "Invitations"
2. View pending invitations
3. Accept or decline invitations as appropriate

## Document Management

### Viewing Documents

1. Navigate to "Documents"
2. Browse folders and files
3. Click on a file to view it

### Uploading Documents

1. Navigate to "Documents"
2. Navigate to the desired folder
3. Click "Upload"
4. Select the file from your computer
5. Click "Upload"

### Sharing Documents

1. Navigate to "Documents"
2. Select the file you want to share
3. Click "Share"
4. Enter the email addresses of the recipients
5. Set permissions (view, edit, comment)
6. Click "Share"

## Email Management

### Composing an Email

1. Navigate to "Email" > "Compose"
2. Enter recipient email addresses
3. Enter a subject
4. Compose your message
5. Attach files if needed
6. Click "Send" or "Save Draft"

### Viewing Emails

1. Navigate to "Email"
2. Select a folder (Inbox, Sent, Drafts, etc.)
3. Click on an email to view its contents

### Using Email Templates

1. When composing an email, click "Templates"
2. Select a template from the list
3. Customize the template as needed
4. Click "Send"

## Notifications and Alerts

### Viewing Notifications

1. Click the bell icon in the top navigation bar
2. View your recent notifications

### Configuring Notification Preferences

1. Navigate to your profile
2. Click "Notification Settings"
3. Configure which notifications you want to receive and how:
   - In-app notifications
   - Email notifications
   - SMS notifications (if enabled)
4. Click "Save"

## Reporting

### Viewing Your Performance Reports

1. Navigate to "Reports" > "My Performance"
2. View metrics such as:
   - Tickets resolved
   - Average resolution time
   - Customer satisfaction rating
   - Leads converted
   - Contract value secured

### Generating Custom Reports

1. Navigate to "Reports" > "Custom Reports"
2. Select report type
3. Set parameters and filters
4. Click "Generate Report"
5. View or export the report

## Best Practices for Employees

1. **Regular Updates**: Update ticket and lead statuses regularly
2. **Thorough Documentation**: Document all customer interactions
3. **Prompt Responses**: Respond to customer inquiries quickly
4. **Follow-up**: Follow up with customers after resolving issues
5. **Use Templates**: Use email templates for consistent communication
6. **Share Knowledge**: Document solutions for common problems
7. **Stay Organized**: Keep your calendar and task list up to date
8. **Continuous Learning**: Explore all features of the CRM system

## Getting Help

If you need assistance:

1. Click the Help icon (?) in the navigation bar
2. Check the documentation for your specific task
3. Contact your manager or system administrator
4. Email the support team at support@yourcompany.com
