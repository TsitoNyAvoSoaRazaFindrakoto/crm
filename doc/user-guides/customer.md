# Customer User Guide

This guide provides comprehensive instructions for customers using the CRM Web Application's customer portal. It covers all the features available to users with the Customer role.

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the customer portal login page
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Alternatively, click "Sign in with Google" if you've connected your Google account
4. Click "Log In"

If you don't have an account yet, contact your account representative to request access.

### First-Time Login

If this is your first time logging in:

1. You'll be prompted to change your temporary password
2. Review and accept the terms of service
3. Complete your profile information
4. Set your notification preferences
5. You'll be guided through a brief tour of the interface

### Customer Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with:

- Recent support tickets and their status
- Upcoming meetings with your representatives
- Important announcements
- Contract information and renewal dates
- Quick action buttons for common tasks

## Managing Your Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click on your name in the top-right corner
2. Select "My Profile"

### Updating Your Profile

1. Navigate to your profile
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update your information:
   - Contact details
   - Business address
   - Communication preferences
   - Password
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Managing Additional Contacts

You can add colleagues from your organization to the portal:

1. Navigate to your profile
2. Click "Manage Contacts"
3. Click "Add Contact"
4. Enter their information:
   - Name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Role in your organization
5. Set their access level
6. Click "Save"

### Connecting Google Services

For enhanced integration features:

1. Navigate to your profile
2. Click "Connected Services"
3. Click "Connect" next to Google
4. Follow the prompts to authorize the connection
5. Choose which Google services to enable:
   - Google Drive for document sharing
   - Google Calendar for meeting scheduling
   - Gmail for email communication

## Support Tickets

### Viewing Your Tickets

1. Navigate to "Support" > "My Tickets"
2. You'll see a list of all your tickets with:
   - Ticket number
   - Subject
   - Status
   - Priority
   - Creation date
   - Last update date
3. Click on any ticket to view its details

### Creating a New Ticket

1. Navigate to "Support" > "Create Ticket"
2. Fill in the ticket details:
   - Subject: Brief description of the issue
   - Description: Detailed explanation of the problem
   - Priority: Low, Medium, High, or Critical
   - Category: Select the most relevant category
3. Attach files if needed
4. Click "Submit Ticket"

### Updating a Ticket

1. Open the ticket you want to update
2. Add a comment in the reply box
3. Attach additional files if needed
4. Click "Add Reply"

### Closing a Ticket

When your issue has been resolved:

1. Open the ticket
2. Click "Close Ticket"
3. Provide feedback on the resolution (optional)
4. Click "Confirm"

### Reopening a Ticket

If an issue recurs after closing:

1. Navigate to "Support" > "My Tickets"
2. Set the filter to show closed tickets
3. Open the relevant ticket
4. Click "Reopen Ticket"
5. Provide a reason for reopening
6. Click "Submit"

## Contracts and Services

### Viewing Your Contracts

1. Navigate to "Contracts" > "My Contracts"
2. View a list of all your active and past contracts
3. Click on any contract to view its details:
   - Contract terms
   - Start and end dates
   - Services included
   - Payment information
   - Related documents

### Service Catalog

Browse available services and upgrade options:

1. Navigate to "Services" > "Service Catalog"
2. Browse services by category
3. View service details, including:
   - Description
   - Features
   - Pricing
   - Terms
4. Click "Request Service" to inquire about a new service

### Requesting Contract Changes

If you need to modify your existing contract:

1. Navigate to "Contracts" > "My Contracts"
2. Open the contract you want to modify
3. Click "Request Change"
4. Specify the requested changes
5. Click "Submit Request"
6. Your account representative will contact you to discuss the changes

## Leads and Opportunities

### Viewing Your Leads

1. Navigate to "Sales" > "My Leads"
2. View leads that have been created for your account
3. Click on any lead to view details about the potential opportunity

### Providing Lead Feedback

Help your sales representative better understand your needs:

1. Open a lead
2. Click "Provide Feedback"
3. Complete the feedback form
4. Click "Submit"

## Document Management

### Accessing Shared Documents

1. Navigate to "Documents" > "Shared With Me"
2. Browse folders and files shared by your representative
3. Click on a file to view it
4. Download files as needed

### Uploading Documents

1. Navigate to "Documents" > "My Documents"
2. Click "Upload Document"
3. Select files from your computer
4. Add a description (optional)
5. Set sharing permissions
6. Click "Upload"

### Document Sharing Preferences

Control how your documents are shared:

1. Navigate to "Profile" > "Document Preferences"
2. Set default sharing options
3. Configure automatic notifications for document updates
4. Click "Save Preferences"

## Meetings and Calendar

### Viewing Scheduled Meetings

1. Navigate to "Calendar"
2. View all scheduled meetings with your representatives
3. Click on a meeting to view details

### Requesting a Meeting

1. Navigate to "Calendar" > "Request Meeting"
2. Select your preferred date and time
3. Provide meeting details:
   - Purpose
   - Attendees
   - Topics to discuss
   - Meeting format (in-person, phone, video)
4. Click "Send Request"

### Responding to Meeting Invitations

1. Navigate to "Calendar" > "Invitations"
2. View pending invitations
3. Accept, decline, or propose a new time
4. Add comments if needed
5. Click "Send Response"

## Notifications and Preferences

### Setting Notification Preferences

1. Navigate to "Profile" > "Notification Settings"
2. Configure which notifications you want to receive:
   - Ticket updates
   - Contract changes
   - Meeting invitations
   - Document sharing
3. Select notification methods:
   - Email
   - In-app notifications
   - SMS (if enabled)
4. Set frequency preferences
5. Click "Save Settings"

### Viewing Recent Notifications

1. Click the bell icon in the top navigation bar
2. View all recent notifications
3. Click on a notification to view the related item
4. Mark notifications as read or delete them

## Feedback and Surveys

### Completing Satisfaction Surveys

Periodically, you may receive requests to complete satisfaction surveys:

1. Click the survey link in the notification
2. Rate your experience
3. Provide feedback on how we can improve
4. Submit the survey

### Submitting Feature Requests

If you have ideas for new features:

1. Navigate to "Support" > "Feature Requests"
2. Click "New Feature Request"
3. Describe the feature you'd like to see
4. Explain how it would benefit you
5. Submit the request

## Mobile Access

### Using the Mobile Interface

The customer portal is accessible on mobile devices:

1. Visit the same URL using your mobile browser
2. Log in with your credentials
3. The interface will adapt to your screen size
4. All core features are available on mobile

### Mobile Notifications

Enable push notifications on your mobile device:

1. Navigate to "Profile" > "Mobile Settings"
2. Toggle "Enable Push Notifications"
3. Select which notifications to receive on mobile
4. Click "Save Settings"

## Security and Privacy

### Changing Your Password

1. Navigate to "Profile" > "Security Settings"
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click "Save"

### Two-Factor Authentication

For enhanced security:

1. Navigate to "Profile" > "Security Settings"
2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Choose your preferred method:
   - SMS code
   - Authentication app
   - Email verification
4. Follow the setup instructions
5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

### Session Management

View and manage your active sessions:

1. Navigate to "Profile" > "Security Settings" > "Active Sessions"
2. View all devices where you're currently logged in
3. Click "End Session" to log out remotely
4. Enable "Notify me of new logins" for additional security

## Getting Help

If you need assistance:

1. Click the "Help" button in the navigation bar
2. Browse help articles and FAQs
3. Use the search function to find specific topics
4. Contact your account representative directly
5. Submit a support ticket if you can't find the answer

### Emergency Support

For urgent issues requiring immediate attention:

1. Navigate to "Support" > "Emergency Support"
2. Click "Request Emergency Support"
3. Provide details about the urgent issue
4. Submit the request
5. A support representative will contact you promptly
